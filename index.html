THE ANONYMOUS

Welcome to C-G@MES! Ever thought of becoming a hacker and hack into other's computers? If you think so then you are at the right place. You are always welcome here. Given here is the game that can simulate your thoughts into gaming experience.

GAMEPLAY

You accept the offer for joining the anonymous group. Next you are contacted for various missions to hack into other's computer. As you hack you level up. You got an AI to assist you. This way as the game advances you get many things to explore and more tools to download. Now as the story advances you get a situation where you yourself get trapped in the hands of another hacker.

Could you escape from him and prove yourself the number one non-traceable hacker ?
