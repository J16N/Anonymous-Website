GAMEPLAY

You are a nice class-1 IT-Engineer. One day suddenly you got a call from an Anonymous Group. You are given an option to choose to join the group. You decided to join the group and helped them to fight against evil. You meet an AI TX001 in your terminal, use various tools and explore different kinds of things. Gradually you gain experience in hacking. When you are about to become a pro-hacker you met someone who is the head of various misdeeds. He damaged your TX001, trapped you and started mocking you. Can you escape him and prove yourself a #1 hacker of the Anonymous group?

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

  • OS: Microsoft Windows XP or later
  • Processor: Intel Core 2 duo / Core i3 or later
  • Memory: 512 MB RAM or more
  • Directx: Version 9.0 or later
  • Sound: Any compatible sound card
  • Network: No Internet is needed to play the game
